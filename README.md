# Financial Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

Interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze company wide financial performance and business KPIs including revenue, profit, cost analysis and regional sales trends.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses require centralized reporting to monitor financial performance, identify profitable regions and products and support data driven decision making.

---

## Objectives

- Monitor revenue and profitability trends
- Analyze regional and product performance
- Identify the impact of discounts on sales
- Improve KPI visibility for stakeholders

---

## Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters for Year, Month, and Quarter
- KPI cards for Revenue, Profit, COGS, and Units Sold
- Regional sales analysis
- Product and segment performance tracking
- Monthly profitability trends
- Discount impact analysis

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel

---

## KPIs Analyzed

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Revenue | Total company sales |
| Profit | Net profitability |
| COGS | Cost of goods sold |
| Units Sold | Total products sold |
| Discount Impact | Effect of discounts on profit |

---

## Key Insights

- Identified top-performing regions contributing highest revenue
- Highlighted products with declining profitability
- Analyzed how discount strategies impacted margins
- Improved visibility into monthly financial trends
